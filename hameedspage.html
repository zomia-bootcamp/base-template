About Hameed

Early Life

Hameed spent his early and middle childhood in Saudi before moving to Pakistan with his family. They lived there for 4 years then moved
to Philadelphia to spend his adolescence there. He went to high school and some college in suburbs of Philadelphia before moving to California
during the '07-'08 recession.

Professional Life

In 2011, Hameed traveled to his homeland of Afghanistan to work with the Department of Defense as a Cultural Advisor. In this role, he was
responsible for building relationships with local leaders and developing programs that promoted peace and stability. In 2014, Hameed traveled to
Kenya where he committed to climate change while working daily to produce prosperity through sustainable small business growth in third
world countries – embracing diversity and inclusion. Adept at creating an environment where people want to work and
a brand affinity that keeps bringing people back.

Diplomatic and tactful, able to build effective relationships with persons at all levels.

Strong. Decisive. Direct.
